Taekwondo

Taekwondo has been a huge part of my college career. Since 2018, I have been training Taekwondo with Columbia Taekwondo Team (CUTKD). Along with my academic journey, I have competed in tournaments hosted by Eastern Collegiate Taekwondo Conference (ECTC) and National Collegiate Taekwondo Association (NCTA) to represent Columbia.

With amazing and inspiring people in CUTKD, I was able to find my place at Columbia and have met the best friends of my life. I was able to get stronger both physically and mentally with teammates, and this is why I love this community so much.

Being able to give back to this amazing community by serving as a Vice President and President of the team was the best decision I made during my college career!

I am currently a second-dan Black belt in WTF Taekwondo, and definitely interested in talking about Taekwondo or martial arts.

If you want to connect, don't hesitate to reach out to jy2908@columbia.edu. You can also connect with CUTKD on its Website or via email - cutkd212@gmail.com!
